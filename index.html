Our Story

Founded in 2009, Nengli Limited began as a traditional content marketing agency with a bold vision: to revolutionize how brands connect with their audiences through the power of storytelling and technology.

With over 15 years of industry experience, we've evolved from traditional marketing specialists into AI-native content pioneers. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, serving 300+ brands across diverse industries and generating over 500 million content views.

Today, we stand at the forefront of the AI revolution, combining deep industry expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver unprecedented marketing solutions that drive real, measurable results for our clients.
